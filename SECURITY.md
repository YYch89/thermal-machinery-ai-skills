# Security And Safety

## Reporting

If you find a privacy leak, unsafe instruction, or misleading validation claim, open an issue or contact the maintainer privately if public disclosure would expose sensitive information.

## Safety Boundary

These skills are workflow aids for AI-assisted modeling. They are not safety certification tools.

Do not use the skills as the sole basis for:

- safety-critical control;
- certified engine or power-system design;
- equipment procurement;
- operational limits;
- experimental procedures involving hazardous hardware.

Any real engineering use requires source-backed equations, validated component data, experiment or benchmark comparison, and professional review.
